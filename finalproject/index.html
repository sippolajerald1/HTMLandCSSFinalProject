What is a naval fleet?

According to Wikipedia a "fleet is a large formation of warships-the largest formation in any navy-controlled by one leader. A fleet at sea is the direct equivalent of an army on land. In modern times the squadrens are typically composed of surface warships, submarines, support ships, ship-based aircraft to conduct naval operations at sea."
